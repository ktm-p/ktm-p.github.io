About Me

I'm Michael, a Sophomore at UC Berkeley. I am pursuing a double major in Pure Mathematics and Computer Science, along with a minor in Data Science. I am also part of Berkeley's Upsilon Pi Epsilon (UPE) chapter.

With respect to academic interests, I am mostly interested in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Analysis for Computer Science. In mathematics, I am interested in studying Algebra, Logic, and Numerical Analysis.

Outside of academics, I enjoy playing the piano in my free time. I am also passionate about teaching: I greatly enjoy helping my peers out, mostly in mathematics on topics such as calculus and linear algebra. I also heavily enjoy using LaTeX — I am constantly adding to my personal LaTeX setup, which includes a .sty file that is used for my course notes, and also macros that helps make TeXing faster.
